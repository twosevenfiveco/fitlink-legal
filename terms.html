2. What FitLink is

FitLink is a connector. After you sign in to your own Fitbit account (provided through Google Health), FitLink reads your activity data — steps, active energy, heart rate, and sleep — and writes it into the Apple Health app on your device. FitLink has no servers, no backend, and no account system; your data never passes through infrastructure operated by us.
